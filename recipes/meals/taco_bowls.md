### Ingredients:
- 1–1.5 lbs ground beef
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1–2 green bell peppers, chopped
- 1–2 red bell peppers, chopped
- 2–3 cloves fresh garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp Penzeys Fajita Seasoning, plus more to taste (or taco seasoning if that's what's on hand)
- 2 cups jasmine rice (dry)
- Juice of 1 lime
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
- Salt to taste
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed (or straight from the can)
- 1 can corn, drained (or frozen, thawed)
- Salsa
- Shredded cheddar cheese
- Sour cream
- 1–2 avocados, sliced or diced
- 1–2 limes, cut into wedges

### Instructions:
#### Cilantro Lime Rice:
1. Cook 2 cups jasmine rice according to package directions.
2. While still hot, stir in juice of 1 lime and the chopped cilantro. Season with salt to taste. (Oil/butter optional — fine without.)

#### Cook Veggies:
1. Heat a drizzle of oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add chopped green bell pepper and red bell pepper.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic. Cook until softened, about 5–7 minutes.
4. Set aside.

#### Cook Beef:
1. Heat a drizzle of oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add chopped onion. Cook until soft and translucent, about 3–4 minutes.
3. Add minced garlic. Cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
4. Add ground beef and break it apart as it cooks. Cook until no pink remains, about 7–8 minutes.
5. Add Penzeys Fajita Seasoning. Stir to combine and cook another 1–2 minutes. Taste and adjust.
   - No need to drain the fat or add water — the seasoning sticks fine and the beef stays juicy.

#### Assemble Bowls:
1. Spoon cilantro lime rice into bowl.
2. Add seasoned beef, sautéed peppers, black beans, and corn
3. Top with salsa, shredded cheddar, sour cream, and avocado.
4. Garnish with cilantro and/or squeeze lime over everything at the end.
