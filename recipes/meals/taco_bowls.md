### Ingredients:
- 1–1.5 lbs ground beef
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2–3 cloves fresh garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp Penzeys Fajita Seasoning, plus more to taste (or taco seasoning if that's what's on hand)
- 2 cups jasmine rice (dry)
- Juice of 1 lime
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
- Salt to taste
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed (or straight from the can)
- 1 can corn, drained (or frozen, thawed)
- Salsa
- Shredded cheddar cheese
- Sour cream
- 1–2 avocados, sliced or diced
- 1–2 limes, cut into wedges

### Instructions:
#### Cilantro Lime Rice:
1. Cook 2 cups jasmine rice according to package directions.
2. While still hot, stir in juice of 1 lime and the chopped cilantro. Season with salt to taste. (Oil/butter optional — fine without.)

#### Cook Beef:
1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook 2–3 minutes until onion softens and garlic is fragrant.
3. Add ground beef and break it apart with a spoon or spatula as it cooks.
4. Cook until no pink remains, about 7–8 minutes.
5. Add Penzeys Fajita Seasoning. Stir to combine and cook another 1–2 minutes. Taste and add more seasoning if needed.
   - No need to drain the fat or add water — the seasoning sticks fine and the beef stays juicy.

#### Assemble Bowls:
1. Spoon rice into bowls.
2. Top with seasoned beef, black beans, corn, salsa, and shredded cheddar.
3. Add sour cream, avocado, and a squeeze of lime.
