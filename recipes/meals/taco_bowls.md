### Ingredients:
- 1–1.5 lbs ground beef
- 2 tbsp taco seasoning (Penzeys or 1 Old El Paso packet), plus more to taste
- ¼ cup water
- 1 cup jasmine rice (dry)
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tbsp neutral oil or butter
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
- Salt to taste
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed (or straight from the can)
- Salsa
- Shredded cheddar cheese
- Sour cream
- 1–2 avocados, sliced or diced
- 1–2 limes, cut into wedges

### Instructions:
#### Cilantro Lime Rice:
1. Cook jasmine rice according to package directions.
2. While still hot, stir in lime juice, oil or butter, and cilantro. Season with salt to taste.

#### Cook Beef:
1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add ground beef and break it apart with a spoon or spatula as it cooks.
3. Cook until no pink remains, about 7–8 minutes.
4. Drain excess fat from the pan.
5. Add taco seasoning and water. Stir to combine.
6. Simmer 2–3 minutes until liquid is mostly absorbed. Taste and add more seasoning if needed.

#### Assemble Bowls:
1. Spoon rice into bowls.
2. Top with seasoned beef, black beans, salsa, and shredded cheddar.
3. Add sour cream, avocado, and a squeeze of lime.
